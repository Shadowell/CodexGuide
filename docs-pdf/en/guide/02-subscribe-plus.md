## Why you need to subscribe

The free version of ChatGPT allows you to experience basic conversations, but Codex functions (including desktop apps, cloud tasks, and multi-agent parallelism) require a paid package to use stably.

|combo|Monthly fee (USD)|Codex availability|
| --- | --- | --- |
| Free |free|Trial only, very limited amount|
| Go | $8 |Limited quota, you can experience Codex|
| Plus | $20 |Complete Codex access, enough for daily development|
| Pro | $200 |5 times the quota of Plus, for high-intensity use|
| Team / Enterprise |Pay per person|Full access, including team management|

For most individual developers, **Plus ($20/month) is the most cost-effective starting point**.

## Method 1: Apple gift card (the most stable, recommended to try first)

Suitable for users who already have an iOS device or are willing to prepare one. The entire process is completed within domestic Alipay and does not require an overseas bank card.

**Preparation items:**

- An Apple device (iPhone/iPad, it can be second-hand and can use the App Store normally)
- Registered overseas Apple ID (select the United States or Singapore when registering, no need to bind a domestic mobile app number)
- Domestic Alipay (can be used normally)


**Operating steps:**

1. Log out of the domestic Apple ID on the Apple device and log in to the prepared overseas Apple ID.
2. Open Alipay and switch the location to "San Francisco, USA". Alipay will automatically switch to the international version interface.
3. Find "Apple Gift Card" in the Alipay function area and purchase $20 credit
4. After successful purchase, copy the redemption code, open the App Store, click on the avatar → "Redeem gift card or code", and paste the recharge
5. Open ChatGPT App, log in to your account, enter Settings Subscription Plus, and select Apple ID balance payment

::: tip
If Alipay purchases are occasionally unstable, you can visit Apple's official website through an agent to purchase gift cards directly, and recharge for several months at a time. No repeated operations are required for subsequent renewals.
:::

## Method 2: Android Google Play (alternative)

The logic is similar to Apple gift cards: switch to the corresponding Google account area on the Android device, purchase the gift card through Google Play, recharge it and subscribe. Some users have reported that this approach is feasible, but the measured stability is not as good as the Apple gift card, so it can be used as an alternative.

## FAQ

**Q: Is the Codex quota sufficient after subscribing? **

Plus is billed by token (starting in April 2026), and normal usage for daily development tasks can usually last a whole month. If the quota is used up, you can purchase additional quota separately, or you can wait until the next month to reset.

**Q: Is there a big difference between Plus and Pro? **

Pro’s Codex usage is 5 times that of Plus, making it suitable for heavy users or scenarios that require a large number of parallel tasks. Ordinary developers should start with Plus first, and then upgrade if they don’t have enough.

**Q: Can I use my existing mobile app instead of buying a second-hand device? **

Can. If your device can use the overseas Apple ID normally, there is no need to buy a second-hand one. It is recommended not to frequently switch IDs on your commonly used domestic account devices. It is recommended to prepare a dedicated device for long-term use.

## Verify after subscribing

Log in to the ChatGPT App or web page, confirm in the account settings that the package is shown as Plus or above, and then enter the Codex entry point to check whether the function is available.
